Consulting

Strategic guidance, early in the project.

Three areas where the right code, accessibility, and feasibility decisions — made before the design is locked in — save the most time and capital.

Senior Living

"I help developers and operators make the right code, accessibility, and licensing decisions early in senior living projects — before they become costly constraints."

Key areas of focus

  • During Due Diligence: select the right code pathway (R-2, R-4, I-1, I-2)
  • Align construction type and cost strategy
  • Integrate licensing requirements from the start
  • Coordinate accessibility across multiple standards
  • Ensure design supports long-term operations and aging-in-place
The result Reduce redesign, control cost, and preserve flexibility as the project evolves.

Multi-Family

"I work with developers early in multi-family projects to align code decisions with density, cost, and feasibility — before the design gets locked in."

Key areas of focus

  • During Due Diligence: optimize construction type, height, and allowable area
  • Evaluate podium and mixed-use strategies
  • Coordinate accessibility requirements tied to funding
  • Identify opportunities to reduce firewalls and system costs
  • Align code strategy with budget and schedule expectations
The result Better yield, fewer redesigns, and more predictable outcomes.

Renovation, Repositioning & Conversions

"I help developers evaluate existing buildings — office, residential, or underutilized assets — to determine the most viable path for renovation or conversion before design and capital are committed."

Key areas of focus

  • During Due Diligence: align existing construction type with target use
  • Use the IEBC to minimize unnecessary upgrades
  • Coordinate accessibility across multiple standards
  • Evaluate multi-family vs. senior living feasibility
  • Identify life safety and accessibility impacts early
  • Align strategy with budget and schedule expectations
The result Unlock value, reduce risk, and avoid costly missteps early in the process.
